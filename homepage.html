What Is RPL?

RPL stands for Software Engineering and is a major in Vocational High Schools (SMK). RPL is a major that studies and explores all ways of software development including creation, maintenance, organizational management of software development and quality management. Not only that, RPL is also related to computer software starting from making websites, applications, games and everything related to programming by mastering the programming language. In essence, RPL will not be far from three things, namely Coding, Design and Algorithms which will be the key to the success of software engineering.
